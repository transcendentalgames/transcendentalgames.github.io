Gotta Go

Our Mission: To spread acceptance and awareness of the LGTBQ+ community through interactive media.

Transcendental Games is a college student operated game studio in Worcester, Massachusetts. Our focus is on creating applied games that represent the LGBTQ+ popultion and educate players on the issues that they face. We believe in the power of games and their ability to touch people.
